PestVault EXTERMINATING SERVICE

Family owned, PestVault Exterminating Service has been serving and protecting the public’s health since 1953. The health and safety of your family is our first priority when we perform our services. Our goal is to provide a service of the highest quality in an efficient and environmentally sensitive manner. In fact, we offer green service for those who are conscious of the environment like us. “Maximum security for your children and pets” is not a catch phrase with us; it’s a way of business.

For your protection and ours, we are licensed and insured. In addition, we are a proud member of the Pest Control Operators of PestVault. To better serve our customers, we always stay up-to-date with all the latest trends and techniques in the pest control field. Call today with any of your pest control issues so that we may have the opportunity to resolve them.
